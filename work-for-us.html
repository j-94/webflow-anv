Pay, training, Success

In society there are some companies that pay their cleaners the minimum wage or less. We, however, do not think this is fair. All of our team members who work in our cleaning division are paid above the government minimum wage for all working age adults and they get a great benefits package too. It is only fair to reward people who work hard with good benefits and good pay.

By having our cleaners work for us, they are committed. We don't have people who just want a job on the side. All of them want to be in this industry for a long time and they get trained so they know how to clean better. This way, we have a cleaner that is more capable and makes an important asset to us!

...and more pay

We offer good pay and good conditions for our cleaners. Most of them have been with us for a long time. They know how our company works.

To ensure that our cleaning team can focus on what they do best, we employ all the latest technology to support them. We monitor performance and offer ongoing training to up skill our cleaners.
